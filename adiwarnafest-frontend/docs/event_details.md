
## Introduction to Adiwarna Fest 2026:

    Adiwarna Fest, supported by Buka Bersama (BUSA), is a vibrant celebration of sports, culture, and community at Xiamen University Malaysia (XMUM) for Indonesian students. Adiwarna Fest, organized by GADPA (Garuda Dwi Pantara), is a competitive sports event featuring basketball, futsal, dance competitions, and a Mobile Legends tournament, culminating in finals and an awarding night that honors teamwork, dedication, and national pride. Supporting the festival, BUSA provides a meaningful Ramadan gathering for students far from home to share joy, warmth, and togetherness over iftar. Together, these events enrich the campus experience, strengthen community ties, and celebrate Indonesian unity, culture, and student life at XMUM.

## Purpose of the event

1. To strengthen unity and togetherness within the Indonesian student community in Malaysia, while fostering healthy competition and sportsmanship through men's 5x5 basketball, women’s 3x3, futsal, Mobile Legends, and dance competition.
2. To honour the achievements of Adiwarna Fest athletes through a vibrant and memorable awarding night enhanced by music and live performances.
3. To provide an authentic Indonesian culinary experience through a one-day bazaar that meets the high demand for traditional flavours among Indonesian students, strengthens community bonding, showcases Indonesian culture through diverse and authentic dishes, and supports future activities through fundraising efforts, while remaining open and engaging to the wider campus community.
4. To empower student leadership and event management capabilities by
   involving students directly in organizing and executing the festival.
